Property services for Metairie homes and businesses

Metairie is our primary service area. Hope Indoor & Outdoor LLC handles lawn maintenance, landscaping, exterior cleaning, lighting, and property cleanups for homeowners, rental property owners, and businesses throughout Metairie — from Causeway to Cleary, Old Metairie to Lakeside.

Warm, wet summers mean grass grows fast and properties need more consistent attention. We offer weekly, bi-weekly, and monthly service schedules for lawns that stay maintained all year, as well as one-time jobs for overgrown properties or pre-listing cleanup.

  • Landscaping & Garden Refreshes — bed cleanups, planting, mulch, hedge shaping, curb appeal

  • Palm Tree & Shrub Care — palm frond removal, ornamental shrub trimming, debris cleanup
  • Pressure Washing — driveways, patios, sidewalks, fences, exterior surfaces
  • Gutter & Window Cleaning — gutter clearing, screen cleaning, exterior window washing
