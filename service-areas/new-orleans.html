New Orleans lawn & landscape

Property maintenance built for New Orleans

New Orleans properties deal with a unique combination of heat, humidity, and heavy rainfall that keeps grass growing year-round and can cause overgrowth to become unmanageable quickly. Hope Indoor & Outdoor LLC provides consistent, reliable property maintenance for homeowners and businesses across the city — from Uptown and the Garden District to Mid-City, Gentilly, and the East.

Whether you need a full-service recurring lawn program, a one-time overgrown yard cleanup, driveway pressure washing, or a complete landscaping refresh before selling or renting, we can handle it.
