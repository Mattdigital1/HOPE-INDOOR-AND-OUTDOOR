Lighting types

Options for outdoor & permanent lighting

Landscape and permanent lighting can range from simple path lights to full architectural installations. The right combination depends on the property, the goals, and the budget.

Path & Step Lights

Low-voltage fixtures along walkways, steps, and driveways for safety and nighttime visibility.

Uplighting

Accent trees, architectural features, and landscape focal points with directional uplights.

Permanent Holiday Lighting

Installed once, used year-round. App-controlled color and schedule without climbing a ladder each season.

Commercial Lighting

Exterior lighting for storefronts, parking areas, facility entrances, and commercial properties.

Outdoor lighting services

Exterior lighting for homes and businesses

Hope Indoor & Outdoor LLC provides landscape lighting and permanent exterior lighting services in Metairie, New Orleans, Lakeview, Kenner, Harahan, River Ridge, and nearby communities. Outdoor lighting can highlight architecture and landscaping, improve nighttime visibility, and give homes or commercial properties a more polished appearance after dark.

Services may include

  • Landscape accent-lighting projects
  • Permanent exterior LED lighting
  • Entryway and walkway illumination
  • Architectural and roofline lighting concepts
  • Residential exterior lighting
  • Commercial property lighting requests
  • Lighting paired with landscape improvements

Year-round exterior lighting with flexible curb appeal

Permanent exterior LED lighting can reduce the need to repeatedly install and remove temporary lighting while giving property owners more flexibility for holidays, events, team colors, or everyday accent lighting. Landscape lighting can also draw attention to plantings, walkways, and architectural details.

Plan lighting around the property, not just the fixture

The strongest lighting projects consider viewing angles, landscaping, walk paths, architecture, and how the property is used at night. We can review the exterior, discuss the desired effect, and coordinate related landscape cleanup before installation.
