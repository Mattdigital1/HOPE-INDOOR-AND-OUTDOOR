South Louisiana plant care

What overgrown palms and shrubs need

The warm, humid climate around Metairie and New Orleans creates fast growth year-round. Palm trees, ornamental shrubs, and hedges need regular trimming to stay shaped, healthy, and proportional to the property.

Palm Tree Trimming

Remove dead fronds, seed pods, and skirt growth to keep palms clean, safe, and properly shaped.

Shrub & Hedge Shaping

Prune overgrown shrubs, boxwoods, and hedges back to a clean shape that complements the property.

Ornamental Pruning

Trim crape myrtles, azaleas, camellias, and other flowering plants to encourage healthy growth.

Debris Cleanup

Remove clippings, fallen fronds, and plant debris left behind after trimming and pruning work.

Palm and shrub maintenance

Control fast-growing plants in a long growing season

Local heat and rainfall can cause shrubs, hedges, and ornamental plants to grow quickly. Routine trimming keeps plants from blocking windows, signs, sidewalks, or entryways while helping the entire landscape maintain a cleaner shape.

Detailed cleanup is part of the finished result

Trimming is only part of the job. We also focus on collecting cut material, clearing loose debris, and leaving surrounding beds and hard surfaces looking orderly. Palm and shrub work can also be paired with lawn care, bed cleanup, or pressure washing.
