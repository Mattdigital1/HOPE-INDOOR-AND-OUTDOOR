Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 24, 2026

Hope Indoor & Outdoor LLC respects your privacy. This policy explains what information may be collected when you use this website or submit a quote request.

Information We Collect

We may collect your name, phone number, email address, service address or area, requested service, project details, and any other information you provide.

How We Use Information

Information is used to respond to requests, provide estimates, schedule services, communicate about active or requested work, improve customer service, and maintain business records.

Calls and Text Messages

When you submit a form and provide consent, you agree that Hope Indoor & Outdoor LLC may contact you by phone or text about your request. Message frequency varies. Message and data rates may apply. Reply STOP to opt out of text messages or HELP for assistance.

Sharing

We do not sell personal information. Information may be shared with service providers that support website hosting, communications, scheduling, or business operations, or when required by law.

No mobile information, including SMS opt-in consent or phone numbers collected through text messaging, will be sold, rented, or shared with third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes. Text messaging originator opt-in data and consent will not be shared with any third parties, except as required to provide the messaging service itself (e.g., our SMS platform provider) or as required by law.

Security and Retention

Reasonable steps are used to protect submitted information. No internet transmission is completely secure. Information is retained only as long as reasonably needed for business, legal, and service purposes.

Your Choices

You may request access, correction, or deletion of your information, subject to applicable legal and recordkeeping requirements. You may opt out of promotional text messages by replying STOP.
