Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 24, 2026

Hope Indoor & Outdoor LLC ("we," "us," or "our") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what personal information we collect, how we use it, how we protect it, and your rights regarding that information when you use this website or submit a quote request.

What Information We Collect

When you use this website or submit a contact or quote request form, we may collect the following personal information:

  • Full name
  • Phone number
  • Email address
  • Service address or general area
  • Requested service type and project details
  • Any additional information you choose to provide

We collect this information only when you voluntarily provide it to us through our website forms or direct communications.

How We Use Your Information

We use the personal information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To respond to quote requests and service inquiries
  • To provide cost estimates and schedule services
  • To send SMS text messages and calls regarding quotes, scheduling confirmations, appointment reminders, service updates, and follow-up communications
  • To improve our customer service and business operations
  • To maintain business and service records as required

SMS Opt-In and Text Messaging

By providing your phone number and submitting our contact form, you consent to receive SMS text messages from Hope Indoor & Outdoor LLC. Our SMS messaging program sends communications related to quotes, scheduling, service updates, appointment reminders, and follow-up messages.

Message frequency varies depending on your interaction with our services. Message and data rates may apply. You can opt out at any time by replying STOP to any message. Reply HELP for support contact information and opt-out instructions. Consent to receive SMS messages is not a condition of purchase or service.

Cookie and Tracking Information

This website uses cookies and similar tracking technologies only as necessary for basic site functionality. We do not use advertising cookies, behavioral tracking cookies, or third-party analytics that profile individual users. Third-party services embedded on this website — including Google Fonts and our SMS platform provider — may set their own cookies subject to their respective privacy policies. You can disable cookies in your browser settings at any time; some site features may not function properly if you do so. We do not sell or share data collected through cookies.

Data Security Practices

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. These measures include secure website hosting, limited internal access to personal data, and use of reputable third-party service providers with their own security standards. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security. We retain your information only as long as reasonably necessary for business, legal, and service purposes.

Information Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information with trusted third-party service providers that support our website hosting, SMS communications platform, scheduling, or business operations — only to the extent necessary to provide those services and subject to confidentiality obligations.

No mobile information, including SMS opt-in consent or phone numbers collected through our text messaging program, will be sold, rented, or shared with third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes. Text messaging originator opt-in data and consent will not be shared with any third party, except as required to provide the messaging service itself (e.g., our SMS platform provider) or as required by law. This no-sharing commitment applies to all SMS opt-in data collected through this website.

Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Unsubscribe from SMS: Reply STOP to any text message at any time to opt out immediately
  • Access: Request a copy of the personal information we hold about you
  • Correction: Request that inaccurate or incomplete information be corrected
  • Deletion: Request that we delete your personal information, subject to applicable legal and recordkeeping requirements
  • Opt out of communications: Contact us directly to be removed from any communications

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at ephhope@gmail.com or (504) 920-4569.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. Continued use of this website after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact

For questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or how your information is handled, contact Hope Indoor & Outdoor LLC at ephhope@gmail.com or (504) 920-4569.
