## Local-market design strategy
The site is designed to overcome common homeowner concerns in this category: unclear service scope, unreliable communication, difficulty requesting a quote, and uncertainty about whether one company can manage multiple exterior-property needs.

The website therefore prioritizes:
1. A phone number and quote action in the header and mobile footer
2. A low-friction quote form directly in the hero
3. Clear residential and commercial service categories
4. Dedicated service pages for search visibility and ad landing traffic
5. A simple three-step process
6. A photo-led gallery framework for real project proof
7. Location-specific copy for New Orleans-area searches
8. Transparent SMS consent, privacy, and terms pages

## Reference-layout adaptation
The structure follows the conversion pattern of the requested reference site, but the colors, typography, copy, branding, service architecture, and content are original to Hope Indoor & Outdoor LLC.
